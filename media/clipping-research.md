## Video Script Rules (LEARNED)
- MUST include "(pause)" between every sentence for slow cadence
- MUST spell out pronunciation phonetically: "(KLAV-ik-yoo-lar)"
- Short sentences, 2-4 words each (staircase method)
- Describe visual overlays in prompt: "bold text overlay: '70,000 CLIPS'"
- Describe physical movements: "leans closer to camera"
- End with intense eye contact, not frozen
- Use --json flag for clean output
- Use --wait --wait-timeout 30m --wait-interval 10s (NOT just --wait)
