FAQ

Questions smart brands ask before clipping.

Do we need to create more content first?
No. We start with what you already have: podcasts, streams, interviews, webinars, speeches, and long-form videos. The first job is finding the moments that already deserve distribution.
Is this just reposting short clips?
No. Reposting is lazy. Clipping is distribution engineering: hook selection, formatting, captions, account strategy, timing, and optimization based on what actually moves.
How fast can a campaign start?
Fast. Once we have access to your content library and brand direction, the first wave can be engineered and pushed while the system is still learning your best angles.
Do you guarantee views?
No serious operator guarantees virality. We guarantee the system: strategic extraction, engineered clips, distribution infrastructure, and continuous optimization.
Who is this for?
Founders, creators, funds, education brands, AI companies, and public figures who already have long-form content but are not turning it into a distribution machine.
